Jack Russell Terrier

The small dog with a big personality

5 things you've always wanted to know about Jack Russell Terriers
🐾 They were originally bred in England about 300 years ago to hunt foxes
🐾 These high-adrenaline dogs are incredibly strong and fast (which some owners may struggle to keep up with!)
🐾 Jack Russells are incredibly loyal and love to meet new humans
🐾 They have a strong desire to work
🐾 Jack Russells can be vocal, however they make great watchdogs
